Round I · Midnight Sun

Round I is about surviving the endless daylight. Move through the many shadow bands, use yoga to recover, and keep your stamina above zero before the next challenge begins. When you reach Round II, solve the Turing gate; when you reach Round III, press R to finish the reflection ending.

Quick guide
  • Round I: move between blue shadows and press E to recover.
  • Round II: reach the Enigma gate to open the cipher pop-up, then type 2031.
  • Round III: collect the three Juneteenth markers, then reach the Memory Cairn and answer the Father’s Day reflection to win.
  • Use ← / → to move and ↑ to jump.
  • Polar bears, spikes, and exposure drain stamina.

Arctic Solstice: Never Set

This prototype is a side-scrolling survival game set in the Arctic Circle under the midnight sun. In some Arctic locations during summer, the sun can remain above the horizon through the night for weeks, creating constant daylight and constant exposure.

Your goal is to cross that endless light using shade, yoga, signal decoding, Juneteenth-inspired freedom captions, and memory. Press start to hear and read a short introduction, then use arrow keys to begin.

How to play
Left and right arrow keys move. Up arrow jumps. Press E in shade to practice yoga and recover. At the SHIFT gate, press E to rotate the code ring until the door opens. Polar bears chase nearby players; ice spikes and bright exposure reduce stamina, while constant strain slowly lowers focus.
